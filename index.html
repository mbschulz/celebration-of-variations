Celebration of Variations

A reunion on the occasion of Michael Struwe's 70th birthday

Turin, 23–24 October 2025

We celebrate Michael Struwe's anniversary with a special two-day conference in Turin. The scientific program will feature talks by a number of his former doctoral students. The atmosphere will be informal, blending academic presentations with the spirit of a friendly reunion.

Speakers

  • Franziska Borer (TU Berlin)
  • Reto Buzano (University of Turin)
  • Luca Martinazzi (University of Rome)
  • Melanie Rupflin (University of Oxford)
  • Mario Schulz (University of Trento)
  • Michael Struwe (ETH Zürich)
  • Bian Wu (MPI Leipzig)
